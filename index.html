About Annapurna Ayurveda

4+ Years of Excellence

A Journey to Holistic Wellness

Annapurna Ayurveda Clinic was established with the vision to bridge ancient Ayurvedic wisdom with modern healing needs. Named after the goddess of nourishment, our clinic embodies the principle of providing complete care that nourishes your entire being.

Founded by Dr. Jyoti Thakur in 2021, our clinic has grown from a small consultation room to a full-fledged wellness center while maintaining the personalized care that defines our practice.

Meet Our Expert

Our Ayurvedic Doctor

Certified Ayurvedic Physician

Dr. Jyoti Thakur

Chief Physician & Founder

Dr. Thakur, with over a decade of experience in Ayurvedic medicine, is a well-regarded expert in the field of holistic healthcare. She earned her Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery (BAMS) from the esteemed Institute of Medicine (IOM), further solidifying her deep knowledge and dedication to the healing arts.

Dr. Thakur's approach combines traditional Ayurvedic principles with modern medical understanding, allowing her to create truly personalized treatment plans for each patient. Her expertise spans chronic disease management, digestive disorders, and stress-related conditions.

"Ayurveda is not just about treating illness, but about understanding the unique nature of each individual and guiding them toward optimal health and harmony."
